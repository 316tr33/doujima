Basic Knowledge of Ohenro

What is Ohenro?

A pilgrimage visiting 88 sacred temples associated with Kobo Daishi in Shikoku.

📿 Dougyouninin: Together with Kobo Daishi 🗾 Total distance: About 1200km 👕 White robe, hat, walking stick (recommended)
Essential items:Name slips, seal book, prayer beads, incense, candles, offering money

STEP 2

Worship Procedures and Etiquette

Worship Flow

Learn the basic worship procedures at temples.

1 Bow at temple gate
2 Purify with water
3 Bell tower (once only)
4 Main hall → Daishi hall
Handle fire carefully! Taking flame from others is forbidden
